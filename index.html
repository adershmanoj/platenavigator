MEXICO

Travel Diary

MEXICO

Mexico

It is human to suddenly crave for a bite of soft-shell tacos, a mouthful of enticing burrito, or a mushy spread of delicious guacamole. But as human, as it gets, it gets difficult to bag all the Mexican essentials to build one of the delicacies for yourself. From choosing the right kind of tortilla for your tacos to even seeking the right chipotle and poblanos. Worry no more, relish and dine at these top-notch restaurants, delicately screened to take you into a Mexican world of quesadillas, tamales, and enchiladas.

If Chipotle was your one stop shop for a Mexican feast, and if you would love a change of ambiance and choice, you are at the right place and for sure are in for a treat. So now that you know that Mexican food is much more than tacos and fajitas, you must be craving to taste these culinary treats. Power to you, seize it and make the best of your day, dial a dish or enjoy a neat dine in at a restaurant, a craving is a craving, never let your stomach growl in vain, go ahead, choose one. Now the only question is " Tienes feria? "
